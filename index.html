about

hackWHS II is the second 12-hour hackathon at Westfield High School on December 8th, 2018. Whether you're interested in computer science, electrical engineering, or philosophy, hackWHS II is a great environment to learn, challenge yourself, and change the world.

frequently asked questions

  • What is a hackathon?

    As MLH describes it, a hackathon is an "invention marathon". Whether you want to work with a programming language or hardware, a hackathon is a great place to create, test, and learn!

  • Who can attend?

    Registration is open for all high school students. If you're a middle school student, you are allowed to register if you are accompanied by a high school student. No experience is required.

  • What if I've never been to a hackathon before?

    That's fine, hackWHS great for first-time hackers, with on-site workshops where you will learn everything you need to know!

  • How much does it cost?

    Nothing! hackWHS is completely free to all participants. While you're here, you'll get free food and free stuff.

  • Is hackWHS safe?

    Yes! By attending out event, all attendees agree to abide by the MLH Code of Conduct. For MLH's Parent's Guide to Hackathons, click here.

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your laptop, phone, chargers, and excitement about learning new things!

  • Do I need to join a team beforehand?

    Nope! We encourage you to meet new students during team formation at the beginning of the event. We recommend teams of 4 students.

  • Will you provide travel reimbursement?

    Sorry, we're currently unable to provide travel reimbursement.

  • I have a question that isn't answered here.

    You can email us at hackwestfieldhigh@gmail.com, or message us on our Facebook page. We'll be happy to answer them!
